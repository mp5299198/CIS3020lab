The Legend of Zelda Fandom

About My Fandom

The Legend of Zelda is a long‑running action‑adventure franchise centered around heroic quests, ancient legends, and the eternal struggle between good and evil. The series follows Link, a courageous hero who rises to protect the land of Hyrule and its princess, Zelda, from powerful threats such as Ganon and other dark forces. Each game introduces new regions, characters, items, and puzzles, creating a rich world filled with lore, memorable stories, and iconic music. My website will explore the major characters, important locations, key items, and the history of the series, highlighting why The Legend of Zelda remains one of the most beloved franchises in gaming.
