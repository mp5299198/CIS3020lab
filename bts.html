How I Planned and Built This Website

For this lab, I chose The Legend of Zelda as my fandom because it offers a rich world filled with characters, locations, items, and lore. I began by outlining the content I wanted to include, such as major characters, iconic items, and important themes from the series. I then created three HTML pages: a homepage, a Top 5 page, and this behind-the-scenes page.

The most important part of this lab was setting up navigation so all pages link to each other. I also focused on writing meaningful content instead of placeholders. This structure will serve as the foundation for future labs where I will add CSS, images, and JavaScript to enhance the site.
