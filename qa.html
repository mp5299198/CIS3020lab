Frequently Asked Questions

The Legend of Zelda series has a long history, deep lore, and many mysteries. Here are answers to some of the most common questions fans ask.

Is Link always the same character?

No. Link is a recurring hero who appears in different eras of Hyrule’s history. Each game typically features a new incarnation of Link, Zelda, and Ganon.

What is the Triforce?

The Triforce is a sacred relic made of three golden triangles representing Power, Wisdom, and Courage. When united, it grants the wish of the one who touches it.

Which Zelda game should a new player start with?

Many fans recommend Ocarina of Time, Breath of the Wild, or A Link to the Past depending on whether the player prefers modern open‑world gameplay or classic dungeon‑focused adventures.

Is there an official Zelda timeline?

Yes — Nintendo released an official timeline in the Hyrule Historia book. The timeline splits into three branches after the events of Ocarina of Time.

Why does Zelda sometimes appear as Sheik?

In Ocarina of Time, Zelda disguises herself as Sheik to hide from Ganondorf and guide Link without revealing her identity.
