Frequently Asked Questions

1. Who is Link?

Link is the main hero of the series, chosen to protect Hyrule and wield the Master Sword.

2. What is the Triforce?

The Triforce is a sacred relic made of three parts: Power, Wisdom, and Courage.

3. Who is Zelda?

Zelda is the princess of Hyrule and the bearer of the Triforce of Wisdom.

4. Who is Ganon?

Ganon is the primary antagonist, often seeking the Triforce to gain ultimate power.

5. Which Zelda game should beginners start with?

Many recommend “Ocarina of Time” or “Breath of the Wild” for new players.

6. Is there a single official timeline?

The Zelda timeline splits into three branches after Ocarina of Time.
