Celebrating Zelda Fan Creativity

The Legend of Zelda community is filled with talented artists, musicians, writers, and creators. This page highlights some of the incredible fan‑made works inspired by the world of Hyrule.

Fan Art

From watercolor paintings of Hyrule Castle to digital illustrations of Link and Zelda, fan art continues to be one of the most expressive ways fans show their love for the series.

Music Covers

The Zelda soundtrack is iconic, and many musicians recreate themes like the Song of Storms, Gerudo Valley, and the Great Fairy Fountain using piano, guitar, violin, and even full orchestras.

Cosplay

Zelda cosplay ranges from simple tunics to elaborate armor sets. Fans recreate characters such as Link, Zelda, Ganondorf, Midna, and the Champions with incredible attention to detail.

Fan Theories & Lore Videos

The Zelda timeline is famously complex, and fans love to analyze it. Many creators produce deep‑dive videos exploring lore connections, hidden symbolism, and theories about future games.
